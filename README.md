# 📦 E-Commerce Shipping & Customer Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes an E-Commerce Shipping dataset using Microsoft Power BI to uncover insights into shipping performance, customer behavior, warehouse operations, and product analysis. The dashboard is designed to help business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify operational bottlenecks, and make data-driven decisions to improve customer satisfaction and delivery efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

An e-commerce company wants to evaluate its shipping operations and customer experience to answer key business questions such as:

- Are orders being delivered on time?
- Which shipment mode performs the best?
- Which warehouse handles the highest number of orders?
- How do customer ratings vary across shipment modes?
- Does product importance influence delivery performance?
- How do discounts and customer care interactions impact delivery outcomes?

The goal is to transform raw operational data into actionable business insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## 💼 Business Insights

- Shipment mode significantly influences delivery performance.
- Warehouse performance varies across locations.
- Customer satisfaction differs based on delivery outcomes.
- Product importance affects shipping priorities.
- Customer care interactions increase when deliveries are delayed.
- Discounts can influence purchasing behavior and operational performance.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Optimize shipment modes with higher on-time delivery rates.
- Improve operational efficiency in underperforming warehouses.
- Prioritize high-importance products for faster delivery.
- Reduce customer care calls by addressing common delivery issues.
- Monitor delivery performance continuously using KPI dashboards.
